About Us

Who we are and what we stand for

Welcome to Formality Clothing

At Formality Clothing, we believe that your appearance is important.

Our brand is dedicated to making our customers look stylish and professional at once.

Founded with a passion for delivering high-quality designs and inspiring fits, combining classic tailoring with modern styles.

Whether it's for business, special occasions, or everyday elegant wear, our fits are made to help you look and feel your best.


History

Formality Clothing is a modern formal clothing brand based in Port Elizabeth, established in 2023.

The brand was created to provide stylish, affordable, and high-quality clothing for elegant people who want to express their identity through stylish clothes.

Starting as a small online business promoted through social media, Formality has grown its customer base through word-of-mouth and local support.


Mission

To make high-quality clothes that match the vibes of our customers so that they can express their unique styles.


Vision

To become one of South Africa's biggest formal wear brands, recognized for our creativity, quality and unique style.
